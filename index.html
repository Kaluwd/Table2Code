About Table2Code

Table2Code is a powerful tool that helps developers convert table data between various formats with ease.

Features:

  • Convert tables to SQL, TypeScript, JSON, and more
  • Smart analysis of data structure
  • Generate mock APIs from your data
  • Visualize data with interactive charts
  • Edit and clean data directly in the app

All processing happens in your browser - your data never leaves your device.
